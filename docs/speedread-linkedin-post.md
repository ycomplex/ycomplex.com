SpeedRead started with an article I kept meaning to read but never did — too long, too busy, always later. Sound familiar?

Then I came across RSVP — rapid serial visual presentation. One word at a time, with a highlighted focal letter to anchor your eye. It felt like a cheat code for reading. I couldn't stop thinking about it.

So I built SpeedRead: a mobile app that lets you import books, articles, PDFs — anything — and read them one word at a time at up to 1,000 words per minute.

I finally read that article. And everything else that had been piling up.
